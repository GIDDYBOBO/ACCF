About A.C.C.F

We are a vibrant, growing church dedicated to sharing the abundant life found in Christ Jesus.

The All Christian Campus Fellowship is the student wing of the All Christian Fellowship ministry (AKA : All Christian World Ministry Outreach Int.) The all christian ministry is simply a place where evangelism and sound teaching of the word are backed up with aggressive prayers which have the efficacy to affect the lives of the beleivers both spiritually and physically as well as sustaining the various activities of the ministry is the driving force .The fellowship with the difference.

Today, we are a thriving community of believers committed to authentic worship, biblical teaching, heartfelt prayer, and compassionate outreach. We believe that God has called us to be a light in our community and to share the message of His abundant grace and love.
